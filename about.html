About Us

Our Mission

Using networks and algorithmic data science to help Cornellians find their ~perfect match~ since 2018

Who We Are

Established in 2016, Cornell Business Analytics (CBA) is a student-run organization that offers business analytics services to on-campus and off-campus clients.

FAQs

+ When will the results be released?
February 13th at Noon
+ How do the results work?
Each person will receive anywhere between 3 and 6 matches, all of which are reciprocal. Your netId will be released to each of your matches
+ How are the matches generated?
Cornell Business Analytics has developed an internal machine learning algorithm to generate the pairings based on maximizing likelihood of becoming a couple. Want to learn more? Contact us or apply to Cornell Business Analytics!
+ What is your tech stack?
Frontend - ReactJS; Backend - NodeJS; Database - MongoDB; Server - AWS; Algorithm - Python Pytorch. Interested in learning more? Contact us!
+ Is my data safe?
Yes! All traffic to the site is TLS encrypted, and all stored data is encoded. That means even we can’t look at it!
+ What about other legal questions?
